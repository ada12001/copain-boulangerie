Our Breads

Every loaf begins with four things: flour, water, salt, and a starter that has been alive for nearly four decades. From that simplicity comes a country sourdough with an open, irregular crumb, baguettes shaped by hand each morning, and viennoiserie laminated over two patient days. Nothing is added that does not belong.

For Every Purveyor
